Assembling includes etching and soldering board for developed design.

> Etching
Done by "ЛУТ" method and Iron(III) chloride.

> Soldering
Used technique is described as follows:
	— Tinning Iron & Surfaces
	1. Clean
	2. Cover with Tin
	3. Solder

	— Merging:
	1. Align surfaces
	2. Melt & Merge

	— Notes:
	> Increasing temperature reduces time to tin. Due to fastened forming of oxides
